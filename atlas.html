Introduction

The PACE satellite will enhance our understanding of ocean ecosystems and atmospheric processes. It provides critical data that can be integrated into classroom learning.

Interactive Learning Modules

PACE offers interactive modules that allow students to explore data in engaging and informative ways.

  • Real-world applications of science concepts
  • Collaboration on global issues
